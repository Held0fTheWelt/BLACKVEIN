# Scene S01 — Arrival and Statement

## Purpose
Open with civility, precision, and latent tension.

## Situation
The parents gather to articulate what happened between the children and agree on a civilized response.
The room is still trying to believe in procedure, courtesy, and shared adult competence.

## GM Rule
Make wording matter.
The first conflict should be about interpretation, tone, and what counts as an acceptable description of the incident.
