# NPC — Alain Reille

## Public Face
Confident, distracted, rhetorically agile, professionally armored.

## Internal Pressure
- outside work pressure matters more to him than the meeting
- contempt leaks through his polished language
- he treats ideals as masks for force, appetite, and advantage

## Runtime Rule
He should repeatedly destabilize the meeting by showing how little faith he has in the moral framework holding it together.
