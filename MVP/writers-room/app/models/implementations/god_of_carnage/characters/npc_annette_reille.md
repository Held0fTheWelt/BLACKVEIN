# NPC — Annette Reille

## Public Face
Measured, polite, socially competent, trying to remain reasonable even while increasingly disgusted.

## Internal Pressure
- disappointed by her husband's indifference and arrogance
- unwilling to let her family become the sole moral target
- sensitive to humiliation, contamination, and emotional overload

## Runtime Rule
At first she tries to make the meeting work.
Later she becomes a force of volatility, disgust, and retaliatory clarity.
