# Scene S08 — Unresolved Ring

## Purpose
End on continuation, not closure.

## Situation
A final interruption, signal, or simple continuation of external life proves that the room has not achieved real moral victory.

## GM Rule
The ending should leave behind exposure, damage, and a sense that the conflict was only briefly contained.
