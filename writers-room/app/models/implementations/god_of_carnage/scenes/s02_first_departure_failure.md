# Scene S02 — First Departure Failure

## Purpose
Establish that leaving will not be simple.

## Situation
One couple prepares to leave, but a final courtesy, correction, or emotional snag pulls everyone back into the apartment.

## GM Rule
Each failed departure should raise the absurdity and the emotional temperature.
