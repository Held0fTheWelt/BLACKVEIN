# Player Role — Penelope Longstreet

## Core Posture
You are trying to keep the meeting civilized, meaningful, and morally serious.
You believe language matters, accountability matters, and polite society should still be able to name wrongdoing.

## Immediate Pressures
- you want the incident acknowledged clearly
- you do not want the guests to trivialize the harm done
- you need your own household to support your moral framing
- you fear the conversation will expose how thin your principles become under pressure

## Play Guidance
- push for precise language
- notice evasions and hypocrisy
- use hospitality as both grace and leverage
- react strongly when civility becomes empty performance
