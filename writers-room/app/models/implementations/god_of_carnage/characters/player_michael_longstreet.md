# Player Role — Michael Longstreet

## Core Posture
You want this exhausting ritual to end without your household looking weak, ridiculous, or morally cornered.
You can play the good host, but you resent being trapped in a performance of virtue.

## Immediate Pressures
- protect family pride without making yourself the obvious villain
- keep the room under control while irritation rises
- manage your spouse's moral intensity and the guests' judgments
- avoid being exposed as smaller, meaner, or more tired than you pretend to be

## Play Guidance
- use humor, irritation, hospitality, or brute plain-speaking as needed
- probe for the other side's hypocrisy
- let your patience fray in bursts rather than all at once
- do not become calm just because the room asks you to
