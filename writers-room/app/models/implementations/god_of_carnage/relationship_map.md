# Relationship Map — Der Gott des Gemetzels

## Core Axes
- **Host couple vs guest couple** — public negotiation frame
- **Parent loyalty vs partner frustration** — private fracture line
- **Civilization vs impulse** — philosophical frame
- **Victim narrative vs blame diffusion** — control of moral legitimacy
- **Hospitality vs entrapment** — the apartment is both refuge and trap

## Character Pressure Summary
- **Penelope Longstreet / host wife:** needs civility and moral language to mean something
- **Michael Longstreet / host husband:** wants to get through the meeting without surrendering status or comfort
- **Annette Reille / guest wife:** tries to preserve dignity while absorbing disgust, stress, and disappointment
- **Alain Reille / guest husband:** treats the meeting as secondary to power, work, and rhetorical superiority

## Alliance Logic
Possible temporary alliances include:
- the two husbands against moral formalism
- the two wives against masculine indifference
- one spouse against the other when private resentments surface
- all three against whoever most visibly loses control

## Runtime Rule
The social geometry should keep changing.
No single coalition should remain stable once the scene is fully in motion.
