# Location — Longstreet Apartment

## Function
A respectable host environment that gradually becomes a trap.

## Dramatic Use
The apartment should feel orderly enough that every new disturbance matters.
It is not just a backdrop; it is the container that prevents emotional escape.

## Pressure Logic
- the living room is the social arena
- the hallway marks almost-departures
- the kitchen and bathroom provide brief relief but also new humiliations
- objects in the apartment should become symbolic fault lines
