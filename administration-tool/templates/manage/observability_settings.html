Credential readiness vs proof

Credential-ready means backend can access Langfuse config/keys. It does not prove required runtime scores were emitted.

Source: observability_governance_routes.py + governance evidence contract

Local trace boundary

A local trace export is local evidence only. Do not claim Staging-/Production-proof unless environment and query evidence confirm it.

Source: ADR-0039 evidence quality rules

Danger zone impact

Disable clears configuration/credentials. This is operationally destructive and should be used only for rollback/emergency posture.

Source: observability_governance_routes.py disable endpoint
