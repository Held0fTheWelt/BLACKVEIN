# Product identity and experience definition

## One product, not stitched subsystems

World of Shadows is one product with one combined identity:

- a player-facing dramatic runtime,
- an authoritative world-engine truth host,
- a backend/admin/publishing/governance layer,
- a structured authoring and revision ecosystem,
- and a proof discipline that refuses to confuse target scope with implemented closure.

It is not generic roleplay chat, not a prose toy, and not only a stack of operator tools.

## Two truths that must be held together

### Enduring World of Shadows target
The broader source lineage still commits the MVP to:

- layered runtime reasoning,
- governed memory architecture,
- emotional/social/consciousness participation,
- world-state and relationship depth,
- recovery intelligence and failure-as-story,
- writers’ room intelligence,
- authoring analytics and live tuning,
- and evaluation/governed revision.

### Active proof-bearing center
The current strongest slice is still:

- God of Carnage,
- world-engine authority,
- publish-bound activation,
- authoritative shell observation and re-entry,
- socially legible multi-turn dramatic response,
- and proof-aware closure discipline.

The MVP must preserve both without confusing them.

## Canonical player promise

The player enters a live dramatic situation through natural language.
The system treats the input as part of the room, the scene, and the social pressure already in motion.
The engine validates and commits what becomes true.
The result is visible as consequence-bearing play, not as raw ungoverned generation.

## What the player experience must feel like

The source family repeatedly converges on the same experience requirements:

- the player can tell what is happening now,
- the room feels socially alive,
- characters feel distinct,
- the player’s act changes pressure,
- later turns still remember what became hot,
- and the visible output feels trustworthy because it belongs to committed truth.

## Why God of Carnage remains central

GoC is not just a famous example.
It is the first slice where the package most clearly binds together:

- authored module truth,
- runtime authority,
- player-visible dramatic pressure,
- socially differentiated characters,
- room/object anchoring,
- continuity across turns,
- and concrete validation evidence.

## What must not be flattened away

The recomposed MVP must not flatten:

- WoS into only GoC,
- GoC into only dialog style,
- AI into runtime authority,
- operators into players,
- or enduring target layers into empty future flavor.
