# How to start a session

This guide is for **players**. Operators should read [Setup and first run](../admin/setup-and-first-run.md) instead.

## Before you begin

- You need an **account** on the player site (see [Getting started](getting-started.md)).
- Your operator must have deployed **frontend**, **backend**, and **play service** so play URLs work.

## Typical flow

1. **Sign in** on the player frontend.
2. Open the **game menu** or **play** entry point (labels vary by deployment).
3. **Create** a new session or **join** an existing one if the UI offers it.
4. Wait until the play surface shows the **current scene** and input area.
5. **Type** your action or dialogue in natural language (see [How input affects the experience](how-input-affects-the-experience.md)).

## God of Carnage

For module-specific cues and expectations, read [God of Carnage player guide](god-of-carnage-player-guide.md).

## If play does not start

- Refresh the page once; check your network.
- Contact your **instance operator**—they can verify the play service and environment URLs.
