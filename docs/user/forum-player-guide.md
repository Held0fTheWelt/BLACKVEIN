# Forum — player guide

How to use the **forum** as a community member. **Moderation procedures** for staff are in [Forum moderation workflow](../forum/ModerationWorkflow.md) and [Admin documentation](../admin/README.md)—this page stays **player-facing**.

## What the forum is for

- Discussing game experiences, story, and community topics.
- Starting **threads**, replying with **posts**, and using **reactions** where enabled.
- Discovering content via **categories**, **tags**, and search (if your deployment turns them on).

## Getting started

1. Sign in on the **player frontend**.
2. Open **Community** or **Forum** from the navigation.
3. Pick a **category**, then **New thread** (labels may vary).
4. Write a clear **title** and body; add **tags** if prompted.
5. Submit and participate in replies respectfully.

## Thread and post basics

- **Pinning** — important threads may stay at the top (set by moderators).
- **Locking** — locked threads do not accept new replies.
- **Editing** — you may edit your own posts when the UI allows; edits may be logged.
- **Reactions** — use likes or emoji reactions as provided; they do not replace thoughtful replies.

## Reporting concerns

If you see spam, harassment, or policy violations:

1. Use **Report** on the post or thread (or contact moderation if no button exists).
2. Provide **specific** context; avoid public call-out threads.
3. Wait for moderator review; repeat reports do not speed up handling.

## Community guidelines (typical expectations)

Your operator may publish a full policy. Generally:

- No harassment, hate, or threats.
- No spam or unsolicited commercial posts.
- Respect spoilers and content warnings when requested.
- Follow moderator directions.

## Privacy and safety

- Do not share passwords or payment data in posts.
- Remember forums are **semi-public** within the instance unless a category is explicitly private.
